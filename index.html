Top three things to do in Trivandrum

Temple

Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple

The architecture of the Padmanabha Swamy temple Trivandrum is worth watching as the Gopuram is plated with gold.

Museum & Zoo

Napier Museum

Museum is grounds to the Trivandrum Zoo,
one of the oldest zoological gardens in India.

A must-visit beach in Thiruvananthapuram Kerala. Very clean, a calm beach that will provide you remarkable experience.
